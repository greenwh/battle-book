# Glossary

In-game terms, alliance shorthand, and event names — defined for new players and non-English speakers.

## Game Terms

**Alliance** — A guild. Up to 100 members. The fundamental social unit of the game.

**Armor Rift** — Titan Roc's pet skill (-5% enemy HP). Useful in most PvP but counterproductive in Bear Trap when the alliance kills the bear easily.

**Bear Trap** — A weekly alliance PvE event where players rally to kill a giant bear. Top contributors get rewards.

**Beast Cage** — The building that lets you tame and manage pets. Unlocks at Furnace Lv. 18.

**BHT** — Common abbreviation for Bear Trap.

**Buff Snapshot** — Some events (notably Alliance Championship) lock in buffs at registration. Buffs activated after registration don't apply.

**Chief** — Your governor character. The player avatar.

**Chief Charm** — Equipment slot that boosts the chief's stats. Upgraded with charm materials.

**Chief Gear** — The chief's six-piece gear set (separate from hero gear).

**Expedition Skills** — Hero skills on the right side. Used on the world map — rallies, marches, Bear Trap, beasts, gathering, Castle/Fortress battles, and State of Power.

**Exploration Skills** — Hero skills on the left side. Used in Arena, Lighthouse, and story chapters.

**FC** — Fire Crystal. The premium upgrade currency once you reach Furnace 30.

**Furnace** — The central building. Determines your overall progression tier (Lv. 1–30, then FC1–FC10).

**Hive** — The cluster of alliance members in one geographic area on the map.

**KoI** — King of Icefield. Internal state competition event.

**Life Essence** — Primary resource on Daybreak Island. Used to purchase and upgrade decorations.

**Lucky Wheel** — Periodic event for acquiring Mythic hero shards. 120 spins per event is the F2P value ceiling.

**MF / Master Forgery** — Additional enhancement tier on Mythic gear. Levels 1–5, each providing significant stat boosts.

**Mithril** — High-tier upgrade material. Used for hero exclusive gear and SvS scoring.

**NAP** — Non-Aggression Pact. An agreement between top alliances to rotate the presidency instead of fighting.

**Polar Terror** — A high-level beast that requires alliance rally to defeat. Key SvS Day 3 scoring target.

**Presidency** — Awarded after Sunfire Castle battle. The president sets state-wide buffs and appoints ministers.

**Prosperity** — Island stat that determines Tree of Life level. Earned by placing and upgrading decorations.

**R1–R5** — Alliance ranks. R5 is the leader; R4 are officers; R1–R3 are members.

**Rally** — A coordinated multi-player attack. The starter is the rally leader; joiners reinforce.

**RFC** — Refined Fire Crystal. Higher-tier version of Fire Crystal, used for major upgrades.

**SFC** — Sunfire Castle. The state's central trophy building. Holding it gives presidency.

**SvS** — State vs State (also called State of Power). Monthly cross-state war event.

**T1–T11** — Troop tiers. T11 is the highest base tier; FC troops go beyond.

**Widget** — A hero's exclusive gear piece (5th slot), unlocked at 1★ Ascension. Provides multiplicative Special Bonus stats.

**Wild Marks** — Refinement currency for pets (Common Wild Marks and Advanced Wild Marks).

## Combat Terms

**Garrison** — Defensive deployment to a building (your city, an alliance flag, the castle).

**Garrison Swap** — Technique for replacing an attack march with a garrison march by timing a recall during the last seconds of a garrison march arrival.

**Joiner** — A player who reinforces a rally. Joins another player's rally.

**Lead** — The player who starts a rally. The rally leader's hero skills and gear matter most.

**Solo march** — A single-player attack with no rally. The player's own troops, no reinforcements.

**Troop Replenishment** — Sending additional troops without a hero to reinforce an existing garrison mid-battle.

**Zeroing** — Reducing an enemy player's troop count to zero, often by sustained attacks. The strongest expression of victory in PvP.

## Alliance Coordination

**Cheat sheet** — A short reference document shared in alliance chat. The Battle Book is essentially a long-form cheat sheet.

**Coords** — Map coordinates. Format: (X:1234, Y:5678).

**Drop** — Either dropping a shield (becoming vulnerable) or dropping a flag (alliance territory marker).

**Shield up** — Activating a defensive shield. Prevents attacks but consumes shield items.

**TP / Teleport** — Moving your city to a new map location.
