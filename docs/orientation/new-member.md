1. **Reach Furnace Lv. 16** — this unlocks SvS eligibility and most alliance activities
2. **Join your alliance hive** — teleport to the alliance territory once you're added
3. **Set up daily routines** — log in for your free chest, gathering, and beast hunts
4. **Read the [SvS Overview](/events/svs/overview)** — it's the most important monthly event
