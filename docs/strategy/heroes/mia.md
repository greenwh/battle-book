# Mia

::: info Quick take
Mia is a Gen 3 Mythic Lancer who will replace Molly when your server progresses into Gen 3. She's not available yet — this page covers the preparation, gem budgeting, and transition plan so you're ready when she arrives.
:::

## At a Glance

- **Class:** Lancer (Mythic)
- **Generation:** Gen 3
- **Acquisition:** Lucky Wheel (~80 days after Gen 2 server enters Gen 3)
- **Status:** Pipeline — not yet available on Gen 2 servers
- **Replaces:** Molly (Gen 1 Lancer)

## Why Mia

Gen 3 Mythic heroes have stronger base stats and skill kits than Gen 1 equivalents. Mia is the direct Lancer upgrade over Molly — better skills, higher stat ceiling, and worth the investment window that Gen 3 provides.

Every generation's heroes are designed to be stronger than the previous generation's. Waiting for Gen 4 isn't practical — you need a competitive Lancer during Gen 3, and Mia fills that role.

## Gem Budget

Lucky Wheel requires approximately **175,500 gems for 120 spins** (the value ceiling — don't go beyond 120).

**Start saving now.** On both accounts:
- Maintain VIP (10k gems/month)
- Pause non-essential gem spending
- Reduce Alonzo HoH purchases to minimum or zero (especially on Beav)
- Stockpile gems from daily rewards, events, and milestones

At a conservative F2P gem income of ~2,000–3,000 gems/day (events, dailies, free sources), reaching 175,500 takes roughly 60–90 days of disciplined saving.

## Gear Transfer Plan

This is why Mia is such a clean transition:

1. **Molly's entire Mythic Lancer gear set transfers to Mia for free** — same troop type
2. Enhancement levels transfer: if Molly's Goggles are +45 MF2, Mia gets +45 MF2 Goggles
3. No economic penalty — zero Ore, zero Essence Stones, zero anything
4. Molly moves to bench or secondary role

This means every resource you invest in Molly's gear between now and Mia's arrival is also an investment in Mia. There's no "wasted" spending on Molly.

## Development Plan

When Mia arrives on Lucky Wheel:

**Week 1:** Lucky Wheel → accumulate shards → target 4★ (120 spins)

**Week 2:** Transfer ALL Molly Lancer gear to Mia. Mia immediately has your best Lancer gear at full enhancement.

**Weeks 3–8:** Push Mia's Expedition skills to Lv4. Her skill kit is Gen 3 — expect stronger buffs than Molly's Snow's Grace.

**Ongoing:** Continue gear enhancement on Mia's priority pieces (Goggles/Boots) as normal.

## Common Questions

**Should I stop investing in Molly now?**
No. Molly is your active Lancer and every gem spent on her gear transfers to Mia. Keep enhancing Molly's priority pieces — just don't push past +45 MF2 where diminishing returns hit.

**What about Philly?**
Philly is a Gen 2 Mythic Lancer. She's benched with no further development planned. Philly ≠ Mia — don't confuse them.

**What if I can't afford 120 spins?**
You can still benefit from fewer spins, but the shard-per-gem efficiency drops sharply after 120. Budget for 120 or wait for the next Lucky Wheel rotation.
