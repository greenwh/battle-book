# Strategy

This section covers the **progression systems** that determine how strong your account is over time. Events test what you've built; strategy pages explain what to build and in what order.

Every system in Whiteout Survival interacts multiplicatively — investing broadly across heroes, gear, pets, research, and Island decorations outperforms maxing any single system. The guides here are sequenced to help you prioritize.

## Daybreak Island

Your island is a permanent stat engine. Decorations provide combat buffs, the Tree of Life unlocks milestone rewards, and Prosperity gates everything.

- [Island Overview](/strategy/island/) — What the island is, how it works, why it matters
- [Building Priorities](/strategy/island/buildings) — Tree of Life, Lumber Camps, Timbermill, Starry Lighthouse
- [Decoration Strategy](/strategy/island/decorations) — Rarity tiers, Prosperity math, combat-relevant picks
- [Progression Guide](/strategy/island/progression) — Phased plan from unlock through endgame

## Where To Start

If you're new to strategy planning, start with the [Furnace 20→30 Guide](/strategy/furnace) — it shows you the road ahead and helps you plan your speedup and resource spending. Then read the [Island Overview](/strategy/island/) to make sure you're not leaving free stats on the table.

If you're mid-game (F22–F26) and want to optimize, the [Decoration Strategy](/strategy/island/decorations) page will help you stop wasting Life Essence on the wrong things. The [Hero Hub](/strategy/heroes/) and [Gear Hub](/strategy/gear/) are good next stops once your Island and Furnace are on track.
